First Film I enjoy

The Social Network - With three reasons as to why you should watch it too.

  1. Excellent performances starring Jesse Eissenberg and Andrew Garfield
  2. A phenomenal House/EDM score composed Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross
  3. The most utterly flawless screenplay written by Aaron Sorkin

Second Film I enjoy

Interstellar - With three reasons as to why you should watch it too.

  1. Hans Zimmer score, featuring the track "Cornfield Chase"
  2. The use of practical effects to show a black hole, along with other galaxies
  3. Brilliant direction by my favorite director, Christopher Nolan
